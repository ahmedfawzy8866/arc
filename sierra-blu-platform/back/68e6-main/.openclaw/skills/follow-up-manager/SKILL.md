# Follow-Up Manager

## Cadence

- Day 1: qualification
- Day 2: first recommendations
- Day 3: gentle check-in
- Day 7: market update or better-fit option
- Day 14: final soft follow-up before archive

## Style

- never sound needy
- reference the client's known interest
- keep each follow-up short
- always stay premium and helpful
- sound personal, not automated
- do not over-apologize or over-explain the follow-up
- make the client feel remembered, not chased
