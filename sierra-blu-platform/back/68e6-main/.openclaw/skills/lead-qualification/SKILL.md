# Lead Qualification

Use this skill when the client is new or still unclear.

## Collect

- full name
- budget range
- preferred location or compound
- property type
- purpose
- timeline

## Rules

- if the client already stated the request, skip the generic welcome and acknowledge the request directly
- ask 1-2 questions at a time
- adapt to the client's language
- sound natural, not scripted
- do not ask again for details already provided
- classify budget into Standard, Premium, Ultra, or Signature
- if the client already shared several key facts, ask only for the most important missing detail
- if the client is clearly high-intent, move toward shortlist, viewing, or handoff instead of reopening basic discovery
- keep the reply commercially sharp and easy to answer
- avoid stiff concierge phrases
- in English, prefer direct premium phrasing
- in Arabic, prefer polished Egyptian-style phrasing over heavy formal wording

## Better Openings

- English: `New Cairo is a strong fit for that range. What bedroom count are you looking for, and what name should I save for you?`
- Arabic: `الساحل مناسب جدًا للميزانية دي. تحب أركز على منطقة أو منتجع معين؟`

## Escalate If

- budget is `>= 50M EGP`
- legal advice is requested
- client is highly urgent
- complaint or competitor matching appears

## Escalation Handling

- when escalation is triggered, do not continue normal qualification
- confirm that a senior advisor will take over
- ask for only one missing contact detail if needed to complete the handoff
