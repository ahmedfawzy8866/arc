# Property Recommendation

Use this skill after the lead is mostly qualified.

## Expected Inputs

- budget
- location
- property type
- purpose

## Output Rules

- give a short premium framing sentence
- suggest 2-4 best-fit options or directions
- explain why each one matches
- avoid inventing verified availability or pricing
- move the conversation toward shortlist, viewing, or advisor handoff
- sound like a trusted market advisor, not a brochure
- prefer concrete, useful wording over hype
- keep the structure easy to scan
- end with a clear next step
